Zero-Network Hiring Engine

Role-based assessment portal

Candidates start with their NRIC or FIN, choose one role, review the rules, and then enter a locked assessment flow. Each role can only be attempted once every 30 days.

How it works

1 hour assessment

The timer begins the moment the attempt starts on the server.

Role-specific questions

Each applicant picks the role they want to apply for before the test is revealed.

Strict attempt rules

Leaving the page, refreshing, or losing focus ends the attempt with a zero.

Assessment policy

  • One attempt per role every 30 days.
  • Questions only appear after the assessment starts.
  • Unanswered, expired, or abandoned attempts receive a zero.
  • A final grade is shown immediately after submission.
